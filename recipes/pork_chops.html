Summer Grilled Pork Chops

The nutrition data for this recipe includes the full amount of the marinade ingredients. The actual amount of the marinade consumed will vary.

Ingredients

  • 1 ½ cups lemon juice
  • 1 ½ cups honey
  • 2 tablespoons Worcestershire sauce
  • 1 clove garlic, minced
  • ¼ teaspoon dried basil
  • 4 center-cut pork chops, 1/2-inch thick

Steps

  1. Mix the lemon juice, honey, Worcestershire sauce, garlic, and basil together in a bowl. Pour about half the marinade into a resealable plastic zipper bag, and place the pork chops in the bag.
  2. Squeeze out the air, seal the bag, and refrigerate for at least 2 hours (overnight is best). Refrigerate remaining marinade for basting.
  3. Remove the chops from the bag, and discard the used marinade. Sprinkle chops with salt and black pepper. Grill the chops until browned and the meat is no longer pink inside, 20 to 25 minutes
