Ginger-Touched Oatmeal Peanut Butter Cookies

Per Serving: 509 calories; protein 9.1g; carbohydrates 57.9g; fat 28.5g; cholesterol 51.3mg; sodium 578.8mg. Full Nutrition

Ingredients

  • ½ cup butter
  • ½ cup shortening
  • 1 cup peanut butter
  • 1 cup packed brown sugar
  • ¾ cup white sugar

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
  2. In a medium bowl, cream together the shortening, butter, brown sugar and white sugar. Beat in the eggs, peanut butter and vanilla. Combine the flour, baking soda, salt and ground ginger, stir into the creamed mixture.
  3. Bake for 10 to 12 minutes in the preheated oven, until golden brown. Remove from the baking sheet to cool on wire racks. Store in an airtight container when cool.
